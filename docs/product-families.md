# Depureco Product Families Overview

Comprehensive overview of all Depureco industrial vacuum cleaner product families, their characteristics, and typical applications.

## Compact and Portable Vacuums

### MINIBULL Family

**Description**: Compact industrial vacuum cleaners with single-phase motors.

**Variants**:
- MINIBULL (standard)
- MINIBULL ACD (combustible dust, non-ATEX)
- MINIBULL H ACD INERT (with inert liquid bath)

**Specifications**:
- Container capacity: 20-45 L
- Motor power: 2.6 kW (3.5 HP)
- Voltage: 230V single-phase
- Airflow: 380 m³/h
- Continuous vacuum: 250 mbar
- Weight: 38-50 kg

**Typical use**:
- Mobile cleaning operations
- Small workshops
- Light industrial maintenance
- Bakery and food applications (ACD variants)
- Additive manufacturing post-processing (INERT variants)

### XM Family

**Description**: Compact industrial vacuums for specific applications.

**Variants**:
- XM 20 — Standard compact
- XM 20 JC — With Jet Clean automatic filter cleaning
- XM 20 OVEN — Hot material extraction (up to 250°C)
- XM TORCH — Welding fume extraction with auto-start
- XM TOOLS — For power tool integration

**Specifications**:
- Container capacity: 20 L
- Motor power: 2.6 kW (3.5 HP)
- Special features: Application-specific accessories

**Typical use**:
- XM 20: General workshop cleaning
- XM 20 JC: Caking dust (cement, plaster, flour)
- XM 20 OVEN: Bakery oven cleaning, hot ash
- XM TORCH: Welding stations
- XM TOOLS: Power tool dust extraction

### M Family

**Description**: Mid-size industrial vacuums for moderate applications.

**Variants**:
- M65/100 (standard)
- M65/100 ACD (combustible dust, non-ATEX)
- M PRO (compact professional)
- M PRO ACD (professional, combustible dust)

**Specifications**:

- Construction site cleanup
- Single-station extraction

**Description**: Compressed-air powered vacuums for areas without electricity.

**Variants**:

**Specifications**:
- Power supply: Compressed air only
- No electric motors

- Maintenance-free operation

**Typical use**:
- Offshore platforms
- Refineries
- Chemical plants
- Metal tanks and cisterns

**Specifications**:

## Selection Decision Tree

```
What are you vacuuming?
│
├── Combustible/conductive dust?
│   ├── In ATEX zone? → DEX variants (1/2D, 1/3D)
│   │   └── Conductive metal? → INERT variants
│   └── Outside ATEX? → ACD variants
│
├── Toxic/carcinogenic dust?
│   └── → XFLOOR 3H (Type H certified)
│
├── Oil/coolant from CNC?
│   └── → RAM OIL family
│
├── Welding fumes?
│   └── → XM TORCH (with auto-start)
│
├── Hot materials (up to 250°C)?
│   └── → XM 20 OVEN
│
├── Bulky scraps (film, fabric)?
│   └── → AS family
│
├── No electricity available?
│   └── → AC family (pneumatic) or BULL 24 (battery)
│
├── Liquids only?
│   └── → WD 132 P
│
├── Multi-station facility?
│   └── → CVS ROOTS (engineering required)
│
└── General industrial dust?
    └── → MINIBULL, XM 20, M65/100, PUMA (by scale)
```
